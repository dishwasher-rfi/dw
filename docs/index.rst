Welcome to dw's documentation!
==============================
.. image:: DW.png
**Dish Washer** (dw) is a software for RFI (Radio Frequency Interference) identification and mitigation on signals collected by single dish radiotelescopes.

*Dw is in active development state, be forewarned it may be buggy!*
